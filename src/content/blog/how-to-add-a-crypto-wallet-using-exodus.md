Whether you are new or experienced, Exodus makes it easy to add and manage your wallets.

Exodus is a **software wallet** available on desktop and mobile devices.

## How to Add a Crypto Wallet in Exodus

### 1. Open Exodus App
Launch the app. By default, popular wallets like Bitcoin and Ethereum are already enabled.

### 2. Go to the Wallet Section
- **Desktop:** Click *Wallet* in the left menu.  
- **Mobile:** Tap the *Wallet* icon at the bottom.

### 3. Search for Your Coin
To add a new wallet (e.g., Litecoin, Solana, Dogecoin):

- Click **Add More** or search for the asset.  
- Toggle the switch **ON** to enable it.

### 4. Access Your New Wallet
You will now see the wallet in the list.  
Click it to view your **Receive Address** (public key).  
You can now send or receive funds.

## How to Receive Crypto in Exodus

1. Open the wallet of your selected coin.  
2. Click **Receive**.  
3. Copy your wallet address or scan the QR code.  
4. Use this address to transfer crypto from another wallet or exchange.

## How to Send Crypto from Exodus

1. Go to the wallet of the coin you want to send.  
2. Click **Send**.  
3. Paste the recipient’s address and enter the amount.  
4. Review and confirm the transaction.

## Backup Your Exodus Wallet

Exodus provides a **12-word secret recovery phrase**. Keep it safe!

- Write it down and store it offline.  
- Never share it with anyone.  
- Use it to restore your wallet if you lose access.
