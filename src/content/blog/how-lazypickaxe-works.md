Cryptocurrency mining can be complex and expensive. LazyPickaxe simplifies the entire process by allowing you to rent mining power instantly—no hardware, no setup, and no technical knowledge required.

## Step 1: Create Your LazyPickaxe Account

Sign up on LazyPickaxe using your favourite social media login.

    src="/assets/step1.webp"

## Step 2: Choose a Mining Algorithm

Select the algorithm based on the coin or cryptocurrency that you want to mine. LazyPickaxe supports most popular algorithms such as:

- Sha256(Sha256Asicboost)
- Scrypt  
- Kheavyhash  

 <img
    src="/assets/step2.webp"
    alt="Choose a Mining Algorithm"
    class="rounded-xl w-full"
  />

## Step 3: Set Hashrate and Duration

Choose how much mining power (hashrate) you need and how long you want to mine according to your mining goals. Pricing updates instantly so you always know your cost before confirming.

 <img
    src="/assets/step3.webp"
    alt=" Set Hashrate and Duration"
    class="rounded-xl w-full"
  />

## Step 4: Select a Mining Pool

You may click **"Add Pool"** directly during allocation or preconfigure your pool from the **"Completed Allocation -> Configure Pool"** page and apply it when booking an allocation.

   <img src="/assets/pool/add-pool1.webp" alt="Select add pool" class="rounded-xl w-full"  />
</div>

> Note: Pools can’t be changed once an allocation is booked.

## Step 5: Make Secure Payment

Complete your payment using supported methods such as:

- Bitcoin  
- Lightning Network  

Once the payment is confirmed, your mining allocation goes live immediately.

You can either **click the “Make Payment” button** during checkout or **pay in advance from the Balance page** and use your available balance to book an allocation.

## Step 6: Track Mining Performance

Monitor your mining activity in real time from the **Allocation Page**, including:

- Live hashrate  
- Mining uptime  
- Remaining rental time  

## Step 7: Monitor mining rewards in the mining pool

Monitor your mining rewards directly in the selected mining pool dashboard. Track earnings, shares, and payouts in real time to understand your mining performance.

## Why Choose LazyPickaxe?

- No hardware or electricity costs  
- Instant mining activation  
- Transparent pricing  
- Real-time performance tracking  
- Perfect for beginners and advanced miners
